Hybrid

We're a tech powerhouse offering full-stack software development services to drive your digital success. Our dynamic team of experts is the perfect partner to kickstart your tech journey.

Role : WordPress Developer

Experience : 6-12 months

Description: We are looking for an experienced WordPress developer to join our team. As a WordPress developer, you will be responsible for designing and developing custom WordPress themes and plugins for our clients. You should be proficient in HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript, and have a strong understanding of WordPress architecture and best practices.

Responsibilities

  • Hands-on experience on WordPress

  • Develop custom WordPress themes and plugins based on client requirements

  • Modify existing WordPress themes and plugins to meet client needs

  • Troubleshoot and debug WordPress issues

  • Collaborate with designers, project managers, and other developers to deliver high-quality projects on time

  • Stay up-to-date with the latest WordPress trends and technologies

Requirements

  • At least 1 years of experience in WordPress development

  • Proficient in HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, Hosting and DNS

  • Having experience with APIs, Payment gateways and Chat integration

  • Strong understanding of WordPress architecture and best practices

  • Experience with popular WordPress plugins such as WooCommerce, Gravity Forms, and Yoast SEO

  • Familiarity with version control systems such as Git

  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work independently and as part of a team

  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
